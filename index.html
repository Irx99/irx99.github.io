Hello and welcome to my porftolio (not my curriculum). On the right you can find all the remarkable games I have work on so far. Website included, it's responsive!

About the games almost all are made with Unity and C# but I also have some experience with Unreal and C++. All of them are different in some way since I have always try to learn and practice new stuff. Some of them were made only by me and others were made in group. Some of them are simple experiments and other are bigger works. My point is that I have been doing games for long time. I am confident that I am more than ready to work in a professional environment. To this day I have not any commercial game under my belt, not for lack of interest but because the difficulty to make one just with nothing.

About me I can say I am one of those they call "Gamer". I try to be aware of all that happens in the video games industries and to play all that call my attention. Besides I watch a lot of youtube videos and some anime sporadically. Of course this does not make me to live isolated or something like that, but it is true my friends lifestyle is similar to mine. It is also remarkable that I am very introverted. I am not good dealing with large groups of person.

I am methodical and patient and I have been playing games for as long as I can remember. For all of that I think I have a lot to contribute to this industry. And I am willing to start as soon as possible.
